Plot Summary

The Cold War may be over, but things are about to heat up again! Oscar winner Gene Hackman (Enemy of the State) teams up with Mikhail Baryshnikov (White Nights) in this fast paced, thrill-a-minute ride that hurtles through Europe, culminating in a death-defying confrontation atop the Eiffel Tower!

When an ex-CIA agent (Hackman) is called out out of retirement to escort a Soviet spy (Baryshnikov) to Berlin for a prisoner trade with the Russians, it seems like business as usual. But when the former adversaries realise they've been double-crossed by their own goverments, they must work together to uncover and destroy the double operatives within both CIA and the KGB to win this intricate and deadly spy game.
