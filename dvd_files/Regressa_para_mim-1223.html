Plot Summary

Who knew that when he ordered the special, he'd get the dish of his life? David Duchovny's (The X-Files) "flare for romantic comedy" (Rolling Stone) and Minnie Driver's (Good Will Hunting) "charm and exuberance" (New York Observer) ignite sparks in this "warm-hearted winner" (Jeffery Craig, Sixty Second Preview) about a waitress and a widower who meet and fall in love. It took a lot of cajoling to get Bob (Duchovny), a recently widowed architect, to go on a blind date, at a quirky Irish-Italian eatery. Once there, he's instantly smitten not with his date, but with the cheeky waitress, Grace (Driver). With unsolicited help from Grace's match-making Grandfather (O'Connor), Bob asks her out. And as their relationship blossoms everything seems to be going great for these two love-birds, until an unbelievable truth is revealed - one that could easily break both of their hearts for good.
An incredible cast including Carroll O'Connor, Robert Loggia, David Alan Grier, Bonnie Hunt, Joely Richardson and James Belushi help make this meeting of destiny a match made in heaven.
