Summary
Sandra (Asia Argento), a former prostitute with a penchant for S&M and narcotics, travels to Paris to rekindle her love affair with debt-ridden entrepreneur Miles Rennberg (Michael Madsen).

The couple have a fiery but short-lived relationship and their last steamy encounter ends in violence. Sandra flees to Hong Kong in search of a new life and a fresh start. However, nothing is quite as it seems, as Sandra finds herself trapped in a deadly game of manipulation.
