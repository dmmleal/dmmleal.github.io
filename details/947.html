Summary
Sharon Stone stars as Gloria, a mobster's tough ex-mistress who spent three years in prison for a crime she didn't commit in order to protect her lover, Tony. Tony promised Gloria enough money to set herself up for life, in exchange for taking the rap for him.

When Gloria is released from prison her first stop is Tony's place. Gloria demands her money which Tony has no intention of paying. In the madness that follows, Gloria winds up robbing Tony and his associates at gun point. Now she is in big trouble and must fight for her life.
