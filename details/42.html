Summary
From the filmmakers of the Academy Award-winning box office hit, 'The Prince of Egypt', comes an all-new animated epic, 'Joseph: King of Dreams'. One of the most critically-acclaimed films of the year, this inspiring musical adventure retells the classic story of a boy (voiced by Ben Affleck) with an extraordinary gift of seeing the future through his dreams. Winner of the 'Film Advisory Board of Excellence', 'Joseph: King of Dreams' is a timeless treasure for every generation, with five memorable new songs, sparkling animation and "storytelling at its best" (Alice O'Neill, 'Los Angeles Features Syndicate'). This family favourite is "destined to join 'The Prince of Egypt' and 'The Lion King' as an instant classic" (Bruce Kluger, 'Us Weekly').
