Summary
James Caan is at his very best as a tough, wisecracking professional thief trapped between Chicago organised crime families and corrupt police in this gripping tale. Michael Mann, who directed Heat, Last of the Mohicans and Manhunter and produced The Insider and Ali, wrote and directed this taut, visually dazzling heist-drama.

Filmed on rain-slicked Chicago streets, Thief captures the chilling suspense and human drama of professional crime with incredibly realistic details... heist technology straight out of Star Wars and dialog with the gutter pungency of a wood-alcohol cocktail. Co-starring country superstar Willie Nelson and James Belushi, Thief envelops you in its tough, doom-laden grip and never lets go.
