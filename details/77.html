Summary
In 'The Private Life of Plants' David Attenborough takes us on a guided tour through the secret world of plants, to see things no unaided eye could witness. He shows us the struggle of plants to survive in a story that is full of extraordinary drama and breath-taking beauty.

Each programme in the six-part series focuses on one of the critical stages through which every plant must pass if it is to survive: travelling, growing and flowering; struggling with one another; creating alliances with other organisms both plant and animal; and evolving complex ways of surviving in the earth's most ferociously hostile environments.
