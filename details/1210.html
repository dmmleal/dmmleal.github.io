Summary
Baz Luhrman's dazzling and unconventional adaptation of William Shakespeare's classic love story is spellbinding. Leonardo DiCaprio and Claire Danes portray Romeo and Juliet, the youthful star-crossed lovers of the past. But the setting has been moved from its Elizabethan origins to the futuristic urban backdrop of Verona Beach.

The brilliant and contemporarty retelling of the world's most tragic love affair makes this widly inventive "Romeo & Juliet" unforgettable.
